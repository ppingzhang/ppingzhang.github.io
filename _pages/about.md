About me
======
I’m now a PhD candidate (since 2021 fall) in City University of Hong Kong under under the supervision of prof. [KWONG Tak Wu Sam](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=_PVI6EAAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=sra) and prof. [Shiqi Wang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=Pr7s2VUAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao), with the research interests in deep image and video compression.
